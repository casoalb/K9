8,1Help on ACT                    Minimum Access:   500
-
SYNTAX:
k9 act #channelname <action to perform>
-
HELP:
Makes K9 preform an action inside the given channel
or in the current channel if none is given.
-
Example:
k9 act #funfactory Barks !
will show on the channel this line:
* k9 Barks !
