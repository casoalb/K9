8,1Help on SND                      Minimum Access: 500
-
SYNTAX:
k9 snd #channelname <sound file> <comment>
-
HELP:
This command Makes K9 Play a Sound file to the channel
and also say a small comment about the file that is
being played or whatever you wish to make him say.
-
EXAMPLE:
k9 snd seducme.wav The Best !
This makes k9 play that wav file and also makes k9 say
* k9 The Best !
