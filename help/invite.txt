8,1Help on INVITE                  Minimum Access:  100
-
SYNTAX:
k9 invite #channelname <nick>
-
HELP:
This command Invites the given nick to channelname
or to the current channel (if none is given).
You cannot use a reason for the invite.
-
EXAMPLE:
k9 invite #chatzone Ragan
This command will invite Ragan to #chatzone
