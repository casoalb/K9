8,1Help on SAY                   Minimum Access:  500
-
SYNTAX:
k9 say #channelname <message to say>
k9 say <message to say>
-
HELP:
This command makes k9 send a message to the given
channel.
-
EXAMPLE:
k9 say I think you shouldn't do that Theresa !
This will make k9 say in the current channel:
<k9> I think you shouldn't do that Theresa !
