
8,1Help on ACCESS                Minimum Access:   0
-
SYNTAX:
k9 access #channelname <nick>
k9 access #channelname [*]
k9 access #channelname 
-
HELP:
Shows the access of nick on channelname.
If NO nick is given, it shows the entire list of that
channel.
-
EXAMPLES:
k9 access #Doghouse or k9 access #Doghouse *
will show the channel master of #Doghouse
k9 access #Doghouse Thread
will show the access of nick Thread on #Doghouse
