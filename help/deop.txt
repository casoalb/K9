8,1Help on DEOP                   Minimum Access:  100
-
SYNTAX:
k9 deop #channelname <nicklist>
k9 deop <nicklist>
k9 deop
-
HELP:
This command removes channel operator priviledges
to the specified user(s). <nicklist> can be one nick,
or a list of nicks seperated by spaces
-
EXAMPLE:
k9 deop
k9 deop Thread Cathar
This will make k9 deop you in this channel !
