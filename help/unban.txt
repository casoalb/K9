8,1Help on UNBAN                   Minimum Access:  75
-
SYNTAX:
k9 unban #channelname <nick!user@host>
-
HELP:
The command unban removes bans for a given
nick!user@host pattern.
To remove all k9 bans use * as the user@host pattern.
You can use wildcards anywhere in the pattern.
-
EXAMPLE:
k9 unban #help *!*@*.aol.com
This will unban all 'aol' users that are eventually
banned in the channel #help
k9 unban *guam*
This command will unban all bans that have the word
'guam' in any place on the ban (* replaces any text).
