SYNTAX: /msg k9 setpass #channel <password>
-
HELP:
This command will set your password on k9 when someone
adds you to k9 access list in a channel.
You can later change your password by typing this command
again with the New Password after you identify first.
To see how to identify yourself on k9 to gain access,
check help on: k9 help auth
