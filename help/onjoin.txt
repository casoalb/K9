8,1Help on ONJOIN                     Minimum Access:  450

-
SYNTAX:
k9 onjoin #channelname <CLEAR>
k9 onjoin #channelname <ADD> <new onjoin message>
-
HELP:
This command set the comment that k9 will say when
people join the channel, like a list of Rules or whatever

you want to put there.
The first Line on Syntax, will erase all comment lines on

the channel.
The second line (with ADD) will Add a new line to the
channel comment. You can use Colors and whatever in
the Text up to the Maximum of 5 lines.
