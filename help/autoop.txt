8,1Help on AUTOOP              Minimum Access:  400
-
SYNTAX:
k9 autoop #channelname <T/F/V>
-
HELP:
This command set your nick auto op or auto voice
flags for the given channel.
A flag of V will Auto Voice you upon entering
the channel.
A flag of T will Auto Op you upon entering
the channel.
A flag of F will remove all Auto Functions from
you.
