8,1Help on GUARDT                  Minimum Access:  450
-
SYNTAX:
k9 guardt #channelname <T/F>
-
HELP:
This command set the topic into strict mode.
(if you use T) so only k9 can be asked to change it.
-
EXAMPLE:
k9 guardt F
This channel will unguard the Topic in this channel.
After this all changes in topic made by channel op's
will be active and isn't changed by k9.
